Commercial blueprint

How this MVP becomes a product and gets sold.

Pilot packaging

Paid design-partner motion

  • Target provider RCM leaders with visible denial backlog and spreadsheet-heavy workflows.
  • Sell a 4-6 week pilot focused on one denial category or specialty.
  • Use weekly CSV uploads, analyst review logs, and dashboard reporting to prove ROI fast.
  • Track time per case, throughput, dollars at risk, and documentation consistency.

Pricing and scale

Value-based expansion

  • Charge a paid pilot fee, then convert to annual SaaS pricing based on cases processed.
  • Expand from denial triage into appeal drafting, root-cause analytics, and auth exceptions.
  • Add SFTP and API intake only after CSV-first pilots prove repeatable value.
  • Keep human approval mandatory until customers trust the workflow and audit controls.
